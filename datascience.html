Professional Data Science Workflow

Every step of the lifecycle is covered in training for building reliable AI systems.

1

Data Ingestion

Extract large datasets from relational databases, cloud storage, APIs, and unstructured web sources.

2

Data Preprocessing & EDA

Handle missing values, outliners, perform feature scaling, and uncover structural statistical patterns.

3

Feature Engineering

Select, combine, and transform variables into optimal features to boost machine learning model accuracy.

4

Model Training & Tuning

Train state-of-the-art algorithms, validate robustness, and optimize hyperparameters to prevent overfitting.

5

Deployment (MLOps)

Integrate your models into scalable web apps using Flask/Streamlit and host them on cloud platforms.

6

Monitoring & Feedback

Track performance metrics over time, maintain model drift protocols, and ensure continuous accuracy updates.
